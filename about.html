About h1labs

h1labs was founded by Carlos Paramio, an expert in web applications development and architecture with more than 10 years of experience. During his freelance time, he has helped to enterprises and startups all around the globe to design and build their projects, spoke at many conferences and workshops about development technologies and processes, and collaborate with the best in the industry to offer the clients an excellent and quick service.

At 2013, he joined the best developers and the best graphic / UX designer in the area to create H1labs, so the union of efforts could help to create our own products and help other companies at the technology aspects to reach profit with their own ideas.

H1labs is composed by:

  • Miguel Michán: Graphics / UX Designer.
  • Carlos Paramio: Full Stack Developer and mentor.
  • Javier Rodríguez: Full Stack Developer.
  • Carlos Rodríguez: Full Stack Developer.
