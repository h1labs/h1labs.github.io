portfolio

We offer research & development services, code and interface audit, process and technology consultation, and expert mentoring. Here are some projects we've work with.

Projects

  • Top Secret Project

    We're currently working on a Top Secret project. We'll provide more information soon.

  • HomenetSpain visit site »

    HomenetSpain is a properties comparison site focused on the Costa del Sol market, with a fast, powerful and easy to use search engine. We provided the software architecture and development from the ground.

  • Voota visit site »

    Voota is a nonprofit association which goal is to encourage citizen participation in politics. Its website is where they pretend that everyone can learn and publish their opinions about politicians and groups. We provided frontend development.

  • Mumumio visit site »

    Mumumio is a guide to buy food online. It keeps a directory of independent shops that sell high quality food and a products' guide where people can share their thoughts and discover interesting stuff. We provided the software architecture and development from the prototype.

  • Kakoe

    Kakoe is a themed chat where participants can identify themselves using their Facebook credentials through Facebook Connect and talk about what they are watching at TV. Their comments are also announced at their Facebook walls and/or Twitter accounts. We provided the software architecture and development from the prototype.

  • ShopiAds

    ShopiAds enables Shopify users to create dynamic advertisements to be used at other websites. The application uses their product collections to fetch and display the desired products. The ads can be customized in color and sizes. This is one of the first available applications at the Shopify App Store.

  • Wanna Wine?

    Wanna Wine is a small application for Facebook to discover new wines. Their users can give the application some ideas of their tastes by reviewing some wines, and then be recommended with others with similar properties.

  • rmbrME

    rmbrME (now beamME) makes it easy to exchange socially-networked information and build real-time intelligence on every new contact you make. The perfect digital business card. We helped to develop the web application and API, and optimize some of the existant code.

  • Prized Collection

    Prized Collection was a popular Facebook game where players could buy or steal friends' photos, place nice and funny frames on them, and become a well known photo collector by obtaining some virtual money. The game was perfectly integrated with Facebook, collecting all the available, public photos and alerting the players using notifications or news feed stories about the actions taken in the game. We co-worked to develop this game, designing the API and the different components in which the application was divided to improve scalability.

  • Algeciras C.F.

    We developed the official website for the Algeciras C.F. (a local soccer team). The site integrated a custom CMS to maintain the site with fresh content: News, articles, events calendar, photos album, history of the club, and more.

  • First 5 Santa Clara County visit site »

    First 5 is an organization that supports the healthy growth and nurturing of children prenatal through age 5. We were hired by Initsoft Web Solutions to generate a Drupal theme for this site from several Photoshop templates.

  • Grupo 3 Limousine Voyager

    Grupo 3 Limousine Voyager is a cooperative society dedicated to public tourist and business transfers. We designed and developed this site, integrated with a custom CMS to update its information, and allowing the visitors to book a transfer service from it.

  • Está de feria

    Algeciras / La Línea está de feria was a site where people could see a map of different fairs, know about the events that took place in the stands, and participate by voting or chatting with other visitors.

  • Algeciras Party

    Algeciras Party is a computer party that exists at Algeciras since the year 2006, organized by the Algeciras goverment. Each year the event becomes more important and people all around the country comes to participate. We designed and developed a site to allow participants to be informed about the events, news, contests, locations, and others.
